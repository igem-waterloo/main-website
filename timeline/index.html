Overview of Our Competition Season

Winter ⛄

General

  • Applications open and new members are selected — see the Recruitment timeline above for specific dates
  • Brainstorm and develop project ideas before pitching them and choosing this year’s project

Lab & Design

  • Lab training
  • Design the organisms

Math & Modelling

  • Train members in the tech and math we use

Human Practices

  • Devise a research plan
  • Locate stakeholders relevant to our project

Spring 🌼

General

  • When the bulk of our project work happens — see below to learn what each subteam does!

Lab & Design

  • Engineer the organisms
  • Perform and troubleshoot experiments

Math & Modelling

  • Develop mathematical models for our system
  • Use results of basic models to optimize our system and labwork

Human Practices

  • Talk to stakeholders
  • Analyze our project from different perspectives (environmental, economic, etc.)

Fall 🍂

General

  • Document our research and results on the iGEM competition wiki
  • Proudly present our work at the Giant Jamboree!

Lab & Design

  • Finish any remaining experiments
  • Document results from all the experiments

Math & Modelling

  • Finish analysis of model
  • Write up results

Human Practices

  • Run any final outreach events
  • Explain how our research and stakeholder conversations informed our project design
